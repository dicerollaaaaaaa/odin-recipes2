Beef Fajita

Ingredients

  • 900g Skirt Steak
  • 3 sliced Bell Peppers
  • 1 Diced Red Onion
  • 45ml Olive Oil
  • 15ml Lime Juice
  • 1.5g Chili Powder
  • 1g Cayenne
  • 3g Salt
  • 1.5g Pepper
  • 2 Minced Garlic

Instructions

  1. Throw protein in a bag and put all the ingredients
  2. Let marinade for a day
